Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

🕐 Overview

Raphel ("we", "our", "us") is a Discord bot operated by its development team. This Privacy Policy explains what data Raphel collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding your data. By using Raphel, you agree to this policy.

💬 Data We Collect

Raphel collects only the minimum data necessary to function. This includes:

  • Server IDs — to store per-server configuration.
  • User IDs — for whitelist management, warn records, and AFK status.
  • Channel & Role IDs — for configuration storage.
  • Moderation records — warns, bans, mutes stored for moderation logging.

🛡️ Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Message content (except temporarily for snipe commands).
  • Voice audio or recordings.
  • Email addresses, personal info, or payment data.
  • DM (direct message) content.

🔓 Data Retention & Deletion

Configuration data is retained while Raphel is present in your server. When removed, data may be cleaned up automatically. To request immediate deletion, contact us through our support server.
